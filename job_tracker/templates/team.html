The Team

Jobbly was created by 3 software engineering students as their third project for their bootcamp.

The idea behind it came from observing the necessity to help bootcamp students to stay on top of their job applications with a visually better organizational tool than the usual Google Sheets document with several different tabs, allowing users to focus on the job search instead of the tracking process.

All three members of the team acted together as full-stack engineers, but each one also had individual responsibilities as well:

Bridget channeled her passion for web development on both the front and back-end, with special dedication on wrangling the S3 resume upload and delete functionality. You can see more examples of her ninja-dev skills on her github page here.
