About Jobbly

Jobbly was developed through the lens of a user's experience during the job application process. Maintaining organization, a productivity flow, and a sense of direction allows job seekers to stay encouraged, accomplished and pragmatic. The volume, time sensitivity, and organization that goes into applying for your perfect job, can be a full time job in of itself - Jobbly is here to ease the load and get you organized to show the candidate you truly are.
